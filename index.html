Built for procedure discipline

The app keeps high-urgency memory review separate from full QRH checklist completion. That helps pilots train immediate actions first, then continue into the full non-memory checklist flow.

Training-use disclaimer

DHC-6 Trainer is a study and training-support tool only. It is not an approved aircraft flight manual, QRH, MEL, company manual, or operational authority. Pilots must use the approved manuals, company procedures, and applicable regulations for actual aircraft operation.
