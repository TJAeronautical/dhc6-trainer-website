PT6A-27 overview

The PT6A-27 is a free-turbine reverse-flow turboprop producing 620 SHP (flat-rated). Air enters at the rear, flows forward through a three-stage axial plus one centrifugal compressor, reverses through an annular combustion chamber, then passes rearward through the compressor and power turbines.
