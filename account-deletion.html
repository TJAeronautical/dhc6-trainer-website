Account and Data Deletion

Use this page to request deletion of your DHC-6 Trainer app account and associated app data.

Developer: TJ Aeronautical
Contact Email: tj.aeronautical@outlook.com
Deletion URL: https://dhc6-trainer.netlify.app/account-deletion.html

How to request deletion

Send a deletion request by email using the button below. Include the email address or sign-in method used in the app so the correct account can be identified.

Request account and data deletion

You can also email: tj.aeronautical@outlook.com.

What will be deleted

  • Your DHC-6 Trainer app account, where applicable.
  • Cloud-synced app profile information associated with that account, where applicable.
  • Cloud-synced training records, progress, quiz results, favourites, and saved app state associated with that account, where applicable.

What may be retained

Limited records may be retained only where required for security, fraud prevention, legal compliance, purchase verification, audit, or legitimate business purposes.

Data stored only on your device can be removed by deleting the app or clearing the app’s storage from Android settings.

Processing time

Deletion requests are intended to be processed within 30 days after enough information has been received to identify the account.

This page is provided for Google Play account deletion and data deletion requirements for the DHC-6 Trainer app.
