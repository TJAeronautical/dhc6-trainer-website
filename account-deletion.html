DHC-6 Trainer account and data deletion

Use this page to request deletion of your DHC-6 Trainer app account and associated app data.

How to request deletion

Send a deletion request by email using the button below. Include the email address or sign-in method used in the app so we can identify the correct account.

Request account and data deletion

You can also email: tj.aeronautical@outlook.com

What will be deleted

  • Your DHC-6 Trainer app account, where applicable.
  • Cloud-synced app profile information associated with that account.
  • Cloud-synced training records, progress, quiz results, favourites, and saved app state associated with that account.

What may be retained

We may retain limited records only where required for security, fraud prevention, legal compliance, or audit purposes. Data stored only on your device can be removed by deleting the app or clearing the app's storage from Android settings.

Processing time

We aim to process deletion requests within 30 days after receiving enough information to identify the account.

This page is provided for Google Play account deletion and data deletion requirements for the DHC-6 Trainer app.
