Changelog

Track DHC-6 Trainer mobile, desktop, live preview, and website release status here.

Live Web Trainer — upgraded

The in-browser trainer at live.html is now a working, scored study tool with seven modules: Procedures, QRH, Flashcards, Performance, Cockpit Drill, a new Logbook, and an interactive 3D PT6A-27 engine cross-section (Three.js, click to learn each section). Flashcards and the PF/PM cockpit drill are scored, and every session is saved to a local logbook with best scores and a focus-areas debrief. Decks expanded to 25 flashcards including fuel/oil/hydraulic/electrical systems, plus a Generator Fail QRH drill. Performance flags MTOW/MLW and tailwind exceedances. Design improvements: card hover effects, smooth tab transitions, and the 3D legend. All content is grounded in the DHC-6 AFM/POH, operator QRH, and FCTM; data stays in the browser. All special characters are UTF-8 (no unicode escapes).
