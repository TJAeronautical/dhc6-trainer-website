Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy applies to the DHC-6 Trainer Android app and related website operated by TJ Aeronautical.

Effective Date: 17 March 2026Last Updated: 17 May 2026Developer: TJ AeronauticalContact Email: tj.aeronautical@outlook.comPrivacy Policy URL: https://dhc6-trainer.netlify.app/privacy.html

1. Introduction

DHC-6 Trainer is a training and study-support application for DHC-6/Twin Otter familiarisation, QRH review, memory items, procedure drills, cockpit study, and systems learning. This app is intended for training support only and is not a substitute for approved aircraft manuals, company procedures, or regulatory requirements.

2. Information We Collect

The app may store training-related information such as study progress, flashcard review state, checklist progress, selected aircraft variant, saved cockpit state, settings, and imported study content. Where cloud sync or account features are enabled, account-related identifiers and sync data may be processed to provide those features.

3. Information We Do Not Collect

We do not intentionally collect sensitive personal information such as race, religion, health information, precise location, biometric information, financial information, or government identification. The app is not designed to collect operational flight data from real aircraft.

4. How We Use Information

  • To provide app features and save study progress.
  • To support import, review, and training workflows.
  • To improve stability, usability, and support.
  • To respond to support, privacy, or deletion requests.

5. Local Storage

Most training content and app state may be stored locally on the user’s device. Deleting the app may remove locally stored information depending on Android backup and device settings.

6. Cloud Storage and Sync

If cloud sync or account-based features are enabled, selected app data may be stored with third-party infrastructure providers used to operate sync or account services. Users should only import or sync training materials they have permission to use.

7. Data Sharing

We do not sell user data. Information may be shared only where necessary to provide app functionality, comply with law, protect security, or use service providers that support the app and website.

8. Third-Party Services

The website is hosted using Netlify. The app may use Android platform services and other infrastructure necessary for app distribution, account handling, analytics, crash reporting, or sync if those features are enabled.

9. Advertising

DHC-6 Trainer does not currently use advertising-based personal data selling. If advertising is introduced later, this policy and Google Play declarations should be updated before release.

10. Children’s Privacy

The app is intended for aviation training and study audiences and is not directed to children.

11. Data Security

Reasonable technical and organisational measures are used to protect data. No electronic system can be guaranteed completely secure.

12. Data Retention

Training data is retained for as long as needed to provide app features, maintain user-selected study state, comply with legal obligations, resolve disputes, or support security.

13. Data Deletion

Users may request account and associated data deletion by using the dedicated deletion page or by emailing support.

Users may manage local app data through app settings, Android system settings, deletion requests, or by contacting support.

15. International Data Processing

Depending on the services used, information may be processed in countries other than the user’s country of residence.

16. Aviation Training Disclaimer

DHC-6 Trainer is for study and training support only. It is not an approved AFM, QRH, MEL, checklist, or operational control document. Always use approved aircraft documents and company procedures for actual operations.
