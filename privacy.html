INTRODUCTION This Privacy Policy explains how DHC-6 Trainer collects, uses, stores, protects, and shares information when you use the DHC-6 Trainer Android application. The app is intended for study, revision, training preparation, and knowledge reinforcement only. It is not a substitute for the official Aircraft Flight Manual, Pilot Operating Handbook, approved QRH, company Standard Operating Procedures, instructor guidance, aircraft documentation, regulatory requirements, or operational decision-making. By installing or using DHC-6 Trainer, you agree to the practices described in this Privacy Policy. If you do not agree with this Privacy Policy, please do not use the app. 2.
